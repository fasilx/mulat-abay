Mulat Abay typeface, is a unicode typeface. It supports Latin based unicode ranges as well as the Ethiopic Unicode ranges.

Mulat Abay attemps to re-imagine the Ethiopic (Geez) fonts by using some of the Latin based systems such as Capitaziation and accents.

Mulat Abay is designed by Fasil Fikreab. To contribute, see github.com/fasilx/mulatAbay
